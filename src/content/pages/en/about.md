**Workshop Riset Informatika (WRI)** serves as a hub for research and development, as well as creativity, for students of the Information Technology Department at Politeknik Negeri Malang. Established in 2012, WRI provides a platform for IT students to deepen their technological mastery beyond the standard academic curriculum.

WRI cultivates digital talents ready for the professional workforce by combining theoretical learning with practical technical experience. We actively build synergy with various elements, ranging from lecturers and experienced industry practitioners to external IT communities, to create innovative and relevant research.
